Kalis, Guardian of the Citadel

Age: Unknown

Origin: Unknown

Cause of Death: Unknown

Kalis was a soldier in a foregone era, a man of such skill that he was given impossible tasks to accomplish simply because those in power wished to see if he could. The more battles he won, the more his wounds mounted - but he noticed that those who tended to his wounds improved upon his body, blessing him with strange ferro-archine prostheses. He did not notice that these were the result of the Emperor of Steel until those around him deemed him a monster fit only for death. After that, he had a new master, and carried out his mission as he did all his others - with more skill than any other.

Anima, Guardian of the City

Age: Unknown

Origin: Unknown

Cause of Death: Unknown

War is no place for the weak. Most would do anything to survive - betray an ally, flee a battle, surrender to a superior foe. Anima had never needed to do the first, for all died too quickly. He never had to do the second, because he craved victory and death in equal measure. He never did the third, until the Emperor took notice of him. The first emissary the Emperor sent to recruit Anima was killed - so was the second, and the third. It was only to General Belakastro that Animus was forced to kneel and become assimilated in the Emperor's forces.

Enna, Guardian of the Wilds

Age: Unknown

Origin: Unknown

Cause of Death: Unknown

There is no race that worships the sun like the Alraune - and there was never an Alraune that worshipped the sun as much as Enna. She spoke to the sun, and the sun spoke back; while none beileved her capable of such a relationship, she proved them wrong. She asked for proof of her devotion, and it kissed her brow, causing her hair and eyes to bloom the amber color of its light. When a harsh winter overtook their lands and the sun vanished from the sky, the other Alraune turned to her. She would be their savior - but as she gifted them with the Mother of Haven's light, she learned that she had turned them into slaves.

Voras, Guardian of the Tower

Age: Unknown

Origin: Unknown

Cause of Death: Unknown

There are few creatures more easily appealed to vanity than dragons. Their desire for authority and worship is rivalled only by their greed. While the events of his upbringing are unknown, for Voras, this obsession common among his kind reached untold heights. The only soul in Agartha to actively seek out the blessing of one of the Great Devourers, it was easy for the Mother of Haven to grant him a place where he would be worshipped above all - just as she wishes for.

Genius, Guardian of the Study

Age: Unknown

Origin: Unknown

Cause of Death: Unknown

There is little more interesting to mortals than the study of life and death. Understanding of disease, anatomy, and the nature of the soul are foremost among any topics discussed at institutions of higher learning. But there are some who remain unsatisfied with even the rigorous academia compiled by centuries of discovery, and they seek to make breakthroughs of their own. Of course, the easiest way to understand death is to witness it directly - by causing it, for example. Genius was obsessed with preserving his own life, and would go to any lengths to do so. By the time he became a lich, he was more than willing to continue the work of the Blessed of Night.

Juri, Guardian of the Depths

Age: Unknown

Origin: Unknown

Cause of Death: Unknown

A blackmsith by trade, Juri was content to let the fighting be done by other members of his family and clan. When they fell in battle, however, those who survived brought back the fallen's bones and weapons to him, insisting they be remembered. When he began experiencing visions of the lives of the deceased, he thought nothing of it. The more he worked, the less of himself remained - until one day, he heard a voice. An unknown individual asking for his expertise in the matter he was most familiar with - bones, weapons, and death. The Blessed of Night barely needed to do any convincing.

Citadel

Citadel Agartha. The seat of power for the Six Guardians. Defended by Kalis, the main door hides the secret they were protecting - only able to be opened when all six gather...

City

The epicenter of whatever betrayal destroyed the castle of Agartha. Even Anima's brutal strength was unable to fully contain the rampage of an entire city.

Wilds

Beyond the walls of the Citadel lie a vast swath of underground forest. So long as Enna was in charge, the soil remained rich and the plants grew aplenty.

Study

The center of Genius' arcane research. Whatever he was working on - and if he succeeded - are unknown.

Tower

Shining high above all the others and visible regardless of where in Agartha one resides, the Tower is where valuable gold and gems were kept under Voras' watchful eye.

Depths

A series of lightless tunnels. How Juri and his workers navigated this place with any sense of efficiency remains a mystery.
